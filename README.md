# 🏙️ Community Hero – AI-Powered Hyperlocal Problem Solver

> **Empowering Citizens. Enabling Smarter Cities.**

Community Hero is an AI-powered civic engagement platform that enables citizens to identify, report, verify, track, and predict community infrastructure issues such as potholes, water leakages, garbage accumulation, damaged streetlights, and other public infrastructure problems.

Unlike traditional complaint systems, Community Hero combines **Google Gemini AI**, **Computer Vision**, **Interactive Maps**, **Predictive Analytics**, and **Community Verification** to help municipalities take proactive action before issues become critical.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Communities frequently face issues such as:

* 🛣️ Road Damage
* 🚰 Water Leakages
* 🗑️ Garbage Accumulation
* 💡 Damaged Streetlights
* 🚦 Traffic Signal Failures
* 🌳 Public Infrastructure Issues

Current complaint systems are:

* Slow
* Fragmented
* Difficult to track
* Lack transparency
* Reactive instead of proactive

Community Hero transforms civic issue reporting into an intelligent, AI-driven workflow.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🤖 AI Image Detection

Upload a photo.

Gemini AI automatically:

* Detects issue category
* Estimates severity
* Generates title
* Creates description
* Suggests responsible department

---

## 🗺️ Interactive Smart Map

View reported issues on a live map.

Features:

* Category filters
* Status filters
* Severity indicators
* Location search
* Color-coded issue markers

---

## 📈 Predictive AI Insights

Instead of waiting for infrastructure failures,

Community Hero predicts future risks using AI.

Examples:

* Water leakage hotspots
* Pothole risk
* Garbage accumulation
* Streetlight failures

Each prediction includes:

* Confidence Score
* Reason
* Suggested Action
* Expected Impact

---

## 📊 Complaint Progress Timeline

Every issue is fully trackable.

Status Flow:

* Submitted
* Under Review
* Assigned
* Work Started
* Resolved
* Citizen Verified
